Keep Learning

Hackademy seeks to develop a better breed of software engineer: competent, responsive, creative, self-motivated, and abreast with the latest technologies. Learn from the awesome people of a premier web and mobile development agency and jumpstart your future.

Open Courses for IT Professionals

With the skills and the best practices that we have mastered, the time has come to impart these to others. The Open Courses reflect Ingenuity’s learning sessions, which we do regularly as part of continuous improvement.

Supplementary Courses for Academic Partners

We are pitching in to help address the challenge of improving the quality of IT graduates and make them more employable. We do this by partnering with academic institutions offering IT education programs.

Coding for High School and Out-of-School Youth

We believe in providing early opportunities for the youth to learn programming as well as acquire attendant problem solving and analytical skills. We are seeking partners and support to keep these courses free.
